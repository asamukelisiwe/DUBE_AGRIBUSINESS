 Who We Are

Agriculture That Works for People and Planet

Born in 2026 from a passion for the soil and a vision for a food-secure South Africa, Dube Agribusiness bridges the gap between the farm and the communities we serve.

We practice responsible agriculture, leveraging modern techniques, indigenous knowledge, and strategic partnerships to produce high-quality crops, livestock products, and agri-supply solutions.

Sustainability

We farm in harmony with nature, minimising waste and preserving the land for generations to come.

Community First

Empowering local farmers, creating jobs, and reinvesting in the communities we call home.

Innovation

Blending modern agri-tech with proven practices to deliver consistently superior results.

Quality

Every product that leaves our farm meets the highest standards of freshness and safety.
