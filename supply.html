 How It Works

Our 6-Step Supply Process

A disciplined, step-by-step process that ensures quality, accountability, and freshness at every stage.

01

Cultivation & Farming

Crops are grown using precision farming techniques — monitoring soil health, moisture levels, and nutrient profiles throughout the growth cycle.

02

Harvest & Grading

Produce is hand-harvested at optimal ripeness and immediately graded by size, weight, and visual quality to match buyer specifications.

03

Wash & Inspection

All produce passes through a rigorous wash, sanitisation, and visual inspection process. Non-conforming items are removed before packaging.

04

Cold-Chain Packaging

Produce is packed in temperature-appropriate containers and stored in our on-site cold rooms until dispatch, maintaining 2°C – 8°C throughout.

05

Logistics & Dispatch

Orders are dispatched via our vetted refrigerated logistics network. Real-time tracking and delivery confirmation is provided for every order.

06

Delivery & Feedback

Produce is delivered to your door with full traceability documentation. We follow up on every order for quality assurance and continuous improvement.
