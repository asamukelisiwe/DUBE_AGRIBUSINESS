 Our Story

Grown from the Ground Up

From a simple belief that South Africa's soil holds the answer to food security, Dube Agribusiness was born, a startup with deep roots and ambitious vision.

 Our Beginning

The Story Behind the Brand

Dube Agribusiness was founded with a singular purpose: to close the gap between the abundance of South Africa's land and the food insecurity faced by millions of its people. We are a startup, but we carry the wisdom of generations of farmers in everything we do.

Starting from a modest smallholding in KwaZulu-Natal, we have grown into a multifaceted agribusiness, farming, supplying, and advocating for a more equitable food system. Our name, Dube, is a call to action, it speaks of rising, of standing tall, of elevation.

Every seed we plant, every harvest we collect, every delivery we make is guided by our belief that agriculture done right can transform communities, livelihoods, and the nation as a whole.

 Our Direction

Mission, Vision & Values

Three pillars that guide every decision, every harvest, and every partnership at Dube Agribusiness.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable, high-quality agricultural produce and supply solutions that uplift communities, support local farmers, and contribute to South Africa's food security, profitably and responsibly.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and impactful agribusiness in sub-Saharan Africa by 2035, recognised for excellence in farming, supply chain innovation, and community empowerment.

Our Values

Integrity, sustainability, community, quality, and innovation. These are not just words, they are the daily disciplines that define how we grow, supply, and serve.
